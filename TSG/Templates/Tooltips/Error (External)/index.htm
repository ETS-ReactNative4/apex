Error Tooltip Dynamic

The tooltip is optimized for screen reader and keyboard only users:

  • Set focus on one of the Focus fields, then press Tab and Shift+Tab to navigate between them.
  • Tabbing away from an active element with a tooltip, or pressing Escape, will close the tooltip.
